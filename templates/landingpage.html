Music in Context

Discover Music in Context

A project by John Tokarowski


Order matters in playlist construction, and professional DJ's are experts at designing playlists.
By combining Spotify's recommendation engine and the style of top DJ's, you can discover new music in context.


This tool will design a custom playlist for you, based on the style of a professional DJ.
You can interact with a visualization of the proposed playlist, and automatially upload it to your Spotify account once complete.

This tool will ask you for Spotify Authentication to view your current playlists, and create new ones. No playlists will be deleted.
